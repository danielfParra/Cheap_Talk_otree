Time Limits

Each round, Player A will have {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds to choose a message. Player B will then have {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds to make a guess. After Player B makes a guess, both players will see feedback for 5 seconds before the next round begins.

Please complete your actions within the {{Constants.TIME_PER_ROUND }} seconds. Failing to do so will impact your bonus earnings.

Here is what happens if Player A does not send a message in time:

  • Player A will receive no bonus for that round.
  • Player B will not receive any message but can still make a guess.
  • Player B's payment will still depend on their guess.

Here is what happens if Player B does not make a guess in time:

  • Player B's guess will automatically become the number recommended by Player A's message. For example, if Player A sends the message "You should guess 5," Player B's guess becomes 5 if they fail to respond in time.
  • Both players' payoffs will depend on this automatically-set guess.

If both players fail to stay within the time limit, both players will receive no bonus for the round.
