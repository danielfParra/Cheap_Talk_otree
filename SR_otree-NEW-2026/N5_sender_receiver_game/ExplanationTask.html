Explain your strategy

We will now ask you to briefly explain how you made your guesses.

Your explanation

Please write clear instructions describing the rule or method you used to convert Player A's message into your guess.

Imagine you are writing instructions for an AI, for instance ChatGPT, that should reproduce your guesses when it sees the same messages. You can think of this as sending a message to someone who will try to replace you and will only see your written explanation and the original messages.

  • Describe your general rule.
  • Be as specific as possible.
  • Do not list your guesses round by round.
  • Do not write your guesses again.
  • Explain the method you used.

How your explanation will be used

After the session, the computer will randomly select 5 rounds from your game.

For those 5 rounds:

  • An AI (ChatGPT) will receive:
    • The message you saw from Player A
    • The original game rules
    • Your written explanation
  • The AI will then try to reproduce your guesses in those rounds using your explanation.

How the bonus is calculated

For each selected round, we compute a score based on how close ChatGPT's guess is to your guess.

  • The closer ChatGPT's guess is to your guess, the higher the score for that round.
  • If ChatGPT's guess is exactly equal to your guess, the score for that round is 100%.
  • If ChatGPT's guess is very far from your guess, the score is lower.
Round score calculation

The formula used is:

Round score (in percent) = 100 - 100 36 × ( Your guess - ChatGPT's guess ) 2

If this value is negative, it is set to 0%.

We then compute the average score across the 5 selected rounds.

Final bonus

You can earn an additional bonus of {{ Constants.EXPLANATION_BONUS }}.

Your probability of receiving this bonus is equal to your average score (in percent).

Example:

  • If your average score is 80%, you have an 80% chance of receiving the bonus.
  • If your average score is 25%, you have a 25% chance of receiving the bonus.

The better your explanation allows ChatGPT to reproduce your guesses, the higher your chance of receiving the bonus.
