Information from a Previous Experiment

Context

During today's experiment, you will observe information that was generated by participants who took part in a previous session of this same study.

In that previous session, participants were assigned the role of Player A or Player B. There is a secret number between 1 and 7. Player A's task was to send a message to Player B, not knowing the secret number. Player A could either send any number between 1 and 7, or delegate the decision to the computer, in which case the message contained the true secret number.

After receiving the message, Player B guessed the secret number.

Your Task Today

Today, your task is closely related to the task faced by Player B in the earlier session. You will read the actual messages that participants in the role of Player A sent in the past. After seeing a message, you will have to guess the secret number. {{ if player.participant.treatment == "Belief" }} However, rather than entering a guess directly, you will report your belief about the likelihood that Player A delegated to the computer (in which case the sent number equals the true number). Based on that belief, the computer will automatically calculate and submit your optimal guess on your behalf. Your earnings will therefore depend on the accuracy of your stated belief. {{ elif player.participant.treatment == "FixBelief" }}

For each message, you will be told that it was sent by one of four people who sent that same message in a previous experiment.

You will also be told how many of those four people let the computer choose the message for them.

This information tells you how likely it is that the message is honest in that round.

{{ else }} Your earnings will depend on how close your guess is to the true secret number. {{ endif }}

However, one key difference is that you will not be matched with a Player A who is currently in the room. Instead, you will observe the messages that Player A participants sent during the earlier session of this experiment.

About the Instructions Below

To help you understand the decisions that the earlier participants made, we will now show you the same instructions that they received. All messages you will observe today were generated under the exact rules described in those instructions.

Because your task shares many features with that of the Player B participants from the earlier session, it is important that you read these instructions carefully.
